About Us

Our Beliefs

We have always been passionate about dogs and have been fortunate enough to share our entire lives with them. The bond between a dog and its family is truly remarkable and has existed for millennia. We aim to preserve the best qualities of dogs through careful, selective breeding. With generations of experience in breeding dogs, we are committed to offering only the most perfect, loyal pets.

With great care, meticulous attention to detail, and unwavering commitment, we are confident that we produce top-quality dogs. We believe that a dog’s core temperament must be naturally good and loving. That’s why we carefully select only kind, affectionate, and tolerant dogs as prospective parents. This way, whether you're looking for a working dog or a family pet, you can trust that we have the right combination and choice for you—both as a family and as an individual.

Our Standards

We operate to the highest standard possible, with four of us handling and caring for our dogs and puppies as a family. They are never left unattended and are constantly monitored. Each of our dogs is housed according to the highest DEFRA requirements. We provide our dogs with above-standard space and enrichment. They are our entire life, and our routine is built entirely around their needs. Being based in the countryside, our dogs enjoy plenty of space within our fields.

Our puppies are raised in a home-like environment. Within their own specialized dwelling, they have access to features such as household noises, heating and cooling, showers, daily deep cleaning, and constant hygiene checks throughout the day. We use tiled floors and only raise our pups on clean vet bedding (not sawdust or any harmful, commonly used bedding). We always show our puppies in the environment in which they are raised and take extra precautions to ensure no germs come into contact with them. Hygiene is of the utmost importance to us. Our puppies are thoroughly socialized because we want to give them the best possible start in life.
